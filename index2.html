High Speed Stringer

ConfirmWare High Speed Stringer is an automated production equipment for solderable single crystal and polycrystalline silicon solar cell strings. It is suitable for 125 and 156 cells in whole and half, and is compatible with 2BB--6BB. The equipment adopts various advanced automation technologies such as PLC, servo, self-developing manipulator and machine vision to realize fully automatic processing from battery sheet loading to battery sheet discharging. The designed production capacity is 4000 pieces/hour.
